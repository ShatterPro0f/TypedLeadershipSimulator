Event and Crisis Templates

1. Event Types
--------------
- Environmental: floods, droughts, fires
- Political: petitions, rebellions, coups
- Economic: famine, shortages, trade disputes
- Social/Cultural: festival, scandal, migration
- Religious: schisms, conversions, rituals

2. Event Probability
--------------------
P_event = BaseRate * product(ImpactFactor_i)
Impact factors include:
- Resource scarcity
- NPC/faction discontent
- Advisor influence
- Cultural/religious tension

3. Cascading Events
------------------
- Triggered when P_cascade > random(0,1)
- Ripple effects affect multiple systems (factions, NPC loyalty, resources, diplomacy)
