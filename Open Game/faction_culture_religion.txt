Faction, Culture, and Religion Rules

1. Factions
-----------
- Formation triggers: unmet needs, social tension, charisma
- Structure: leaders, influencers, base, secret wings
- Loyalty scoring: based on NPC attitudes, faction power, and cultural/religious alignment
- Strength: sum of NPC loyalty * capability
- Emergence: probability increases with discontent
- Actions: petitions, sabotage, propaganda, rebellion

2. Culture
----------
- Evolves dynamically: food, rituals, art, family, architecture, honor/shame
- Cultural change influenced by NPC emotions, player decisions, faction pressure
- Emergent traditions form organically

3. Religion
-----------
- Evolution stages: superstition → rituals → priesthood → doctrine → schisms
- Influences morality, social obligations, festivals, politics
- Reacts to crises, player actions, and faction activity
- Spread determined by attendance, player favor, crisis impact
