Simulation Loop & System Interaction

1. Receive Issue
2. Player Typed Decision
3. LLM Interpretation
4. Simulation Update:
   - NPCs (emotions, loyalty, actions)
   - Advisors (influence, advice)
   - Factions (formation, strength, actions)
   - Resources (production, allocation, consumption)
   - Culture and Religion (evolution)
   - Diplomacy (relations, negotiations)
5. Narrative Feedback (LLM)
6. Context/History Update
7. Emergent Follow-Ups

Loop repeats continuously for emergent gameplay
